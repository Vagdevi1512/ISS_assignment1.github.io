Ice Cream

Vanilla

The timeless classic loved by many, often used as a base for various toppings and mix-ins.

Chocolate

A rich and indulgent flavor, chocolate ice cream can vary from milk chocolate to dark chocolate, appealing to different taste preferences.

Strawberry

Sweet and refreshing, strawberry ice cream is a fruity favorite, often containing real chunks of strawberries.

Mint Chocolate Chip

A perfect blend of cool mint and chocolate chips, offering a refreshing and satisfying combination.

Cookies and Cream

Crushed chocolate cookies mixed into a vanilla base, creating a delightful texture and flavor contrast.

Butter Pecan

A buttery ice cream with the crunch of pecans, providing a unique and nutty taste.

Rocky Road

Chocolate ice cream combined with marshmallows, nuts (usually almonds), and sometimes chunks of chocolate, creating a rocky and delicious road.

Pistachio

A nutty and subtly sweet flavor, pistachio ice cream is a unique choice for those who enjoy a hint of nuttiness.

Salted Caramel

Sweet and salty perfection, salted caramel ice cream features the rich combination of caramel swirls and a touch of salt.

Coffee

A treat for coffee lovers, coffee-flavored ice cream often mimics the robust taste of a good cup of java.

Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Vanilla ice cream with chunks of raw cookie dough, providing a playful and satisfying combination.

Maple Walnut

A delicious blend of maple-flavored ice cream with crunchy walnuts, offering a taste reminiscent of a stack of pancakes with maple syrup.

Lavender Honey

An elegant and floral option, lavender honey ice cream combines the aromatic essence of lavender with the sweetness of honey.

Matcha Green Tea

A popular choice in Asian-inspired desserts, matcha green tea ice cream offers a distinctive and slightly earthy flavor.

Tiramisu

Inspired by the Italian dessert, tiramisu ice cream features the flavors of coffee, cocoa, and mascarpone.

Red Velvet

A dessert-inspired flavor, red velvet ice cream often includes hints of cocoa and cream cheese.
